BBQLA

Address: 2315 Jesse Street, Los Angeles, CA 90023
Gallerist: Timo Fahler
Owner: GLASS,EDDIE & SHIRLEY TR

In 2015, Midwestern transplants Adam Beris, Thomas Linder and Timo Fahler pivoted their weekly backyard barbecues in Silver Lake into an art gallery called BBQLA. In 2016, it was listed as one of Los Angeles’ Best Alternative Art Galleries by W Magazine. By January 2017, one of the founders, Thomas Linder had a solo show there. In April,  Linder was featured in the New York Times talking about an outfit he wore to neighboring gallery, Venus Over Los Angeles’ opening. In May 2017, an article came out in Newsweek referencing a protest at BBQLA where activists threw laundry detergent on people gathered there for an art opening, reportedly ruining art. In 2018, this statement went up on their now-defunct website: http://bbqla.net/ But they have recently created a new website and continue to operate. https://www.bbqla.art/
